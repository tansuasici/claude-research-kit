## Formatting
- **Reference style:** <ACS | IEEE | APA 7 | Nature | Vancouver>
- **Citation command:** <\cite | \citep/\citet (natbib) | \autocite (biblatex)>
- **Numbers:** <sig figs policy; units (SI); ranges as "5–10">
- **Abbreviations:** define on first use, then abbreviate; list in <table/nomenclature>

## Structural conventions
- **Section order:** <IMRaD | venue-specific>
- **Abstract:** <structured (Background/Methods/Results/Conclusions) | unstructured>; <word limit>
- **Display items:** <max count>; every figure/table referenced in text

## Do / Don't
- Do: topic sentence first; given-before-new; one claim per paragraph.
- Don't: throat-clearing openers ("It is well known that…"), undefined acronyms, synonym drift.
